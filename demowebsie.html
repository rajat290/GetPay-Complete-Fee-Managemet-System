Everything you need

Powerful tools for every
part of your institution

From fee collection to staff management, GetPay Education is built to handle the full lifecycle of institutional operations — with enterprise security at every step.

💳
Online & Offline Fee Collection
Accept payments via Razorpay, UPI, NEFT, or record offline cash/cheque payments. Every payment is tracked, verified, and reconciled automatically.
🧾
Instant Branded Receipts
Generate and deliver beautiful, branded PDF receipts to parents instantly. Customizable with your institution's logo, address, and seal.
📊
Real-time Reports & Analytics
Daily, monthly, and class-wise collection reports. Defaulter lists, due summaries, and Razorpay reconciliation — all exportable to Excel/PDF.
👥
Staff Roles & Permissions
Assign fine-grained permissions to accountants, teachers, and administrators. Each staff member sees only what they're authorized to access.
📅
Fee Plans & Installments
Create flexible fee structures with multiple installments, due dates, and late fees. Bulk assign to entire classes in seconds.
🔔
Automated Reminders
Schedule SMS and email reminder campaigns to parents. Custom messages, targeted delivery, and campaign tracking built-in.
🛡️
Audit Trails & Compliance
Every action is logged with a timestamp, IP address, and user identity. Full audit history for financial compliance and security reviews.
🏢
Multi-branch Support
Manage multiple campuses from a single account. Each branch has its own settings, staff, and data — fully isolated and secure.
📥
Bulk Student Import
Upload your entire student database via Excel or CSV in minutes. Automatic validation catches errors before import so you start clean.
